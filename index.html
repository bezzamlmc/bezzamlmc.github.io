About Me

I am a software developer based in Silicon Valley.

I have a Computer Science degree from the University of Buenos Aires.

I am always up for a challenge. I grew up watching scinence ficition: Start Trek, Star Wars, Lost in Space ...

I went far and beyond. I started my career setting up computational resources and writing algorithms for economists in Argentina.
In Italy I taught physicists from all over the world to use software to build lab tools along CERN engineers.
In Silicon Valley I joined an engineering company and worked on virtual prototyping for oil exploration, medical ultrasound and NDE.
I touched all aspects of software development from design to deployment and collaborated with teams around the globe including Scotland and India.
